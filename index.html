About

The paper presents the conceptual design of high-speed hypersonic aircraft. The study focuses on the Aurora SR-91, "the plane that doesnt exist," aircraft. The input to this conceptual design is a mission profile. Mission profile is a flight profile of the aircraft defined by the customer. This paper gives the SR-91 aircraft mission profile specified by the US air force. Mission profile helps in defining the attributes of the aircraft such as wing profile, engine design and configuration, propulsion system, etc. We'll be having a further look into the propulsion system which will help us understand the engine design and configuration. A propulsion system directly influences the performance of the aircraft.
